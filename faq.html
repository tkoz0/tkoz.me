FAQ: (in)frequently asked questions

Why does this website look so bad?

Because I prefer the "simplicity" in appearance of very basic HTML/CSS and pages that load fast. I prefer to focus on the content of my pages more than the layout and appearance.

One website that inspired my preference for "older" style web pages is http://numberworld.org/, from the author of y-cruncher. It is not modern looking, rather focusing on content about how to compute Pi and other numerical constants to very high precision. The style has not changed since 2011 when I first found this site, but its content interests me a lot, which is much more important than its appearance.
